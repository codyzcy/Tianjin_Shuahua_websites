偏硼酸钡Barium Metaborate

偏硼酸钡，又称二硼四氧化钡，CAS 号 13701-59-2，适用于船底漆防腐、涂料添加剂以及对钡盐功能材料稳定性有要求的工业应用。Barium Metaborate, also known as Barium Diboron Tetraoxide, CAS No. 13701-59-2, is used for anti-corrosive marine bottom paint, coating additives, and industrial applications requiring stable barium salt functional materials.

产品介绍Product Introduction

偏硼酸钡是一种钡盐类化工产品，技术指标覆盖氧化钡、三氧化二硼、二氧化硅、细度、水溶物、悬浮液 pH、挥发物和吸油量等项目。产品可根据客户需求安排不同包装形式。Barium Metaborate is a barium salt chemical product. Its technical indicators include barium oxide, diboron trioxide, silicon dioxide, fineness, water-soluble substances, suspension pH, volatile matter, and oil absorbability. Packing can be arranged according to customer requirements.

包装、搬运与储存Packing, Handling & Storage

通常采用 25kg 净重塑料、纸塑复合、多层纸袋并配塑料内衬包装，也可根据用户要求安排不同包装。按海运 6 Class - UN1564 危险品配载管理。储存于阴凉、干燥、通风处，保持容器密闭，防止物理损坏，并与不相容物质隔离。Usually packed in 25kg net plastic, paper-plastic composite, or multi-layer paper bags with plastic liner. Other packing can be arranged according to user requirements. Managed as 6 Class - UN1564 dangerous goods for sea transportation. Store in a tightly closed container in a cool, dry, ventilated area, protect against physical damage, and isolate from incompatible substances.
