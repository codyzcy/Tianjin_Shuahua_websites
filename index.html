关于书化About Shuhua

扎根天津滨海，服务海外客户Rooted in Tianjin, Serving Overseas Customers

天津市书华化工有限公司坐落于美丽的北方滨海城市天津，成立于 2003 年，技术传承于天津东风化工厂，是较早从事中国钡盐生产与应用服务的企业体系之一。公司围绕偏硼酸钡、一水氢氧化钡、八水氢氧化钡等产品，持续为涂料防腐、钡盐制造、玻璃陶瓷、水软化及相关工业配套客户提供稳定供应。经过二十多年的市场洗礼，我们始终秉持质量第一、服务第一、责任第一的原则，重视产品指标、包装运输、出口配载和客户沟通，产品 90% 出口海外，广泛受到用户好评。 Tianjin Shuhua Chemical Co., Ltd. is located in Tianjin, a beautiful coastal city in northern China. Founded in 2003, the company inherits technical experience from Tianjin Dongfeng Chemical Factory, one of the earlier enterprise systems engaged in barium salt production and application services in China. Focusing on Barium Metaborate, Barium Hydroxide Monohydrate, and Barium Hydroxide Octahydrate, we provide stable supply for coating anti-corrosion, barium salt manufacturing, glass and ceramic, water softening, and related industrial customers. With more than two decades of market experience, we adhere to quality first, service first, and responsibility first, with attention to product specifications, packing, transportation, export handling, and customer communication. 90% of our products are exported overseas and widely recognized by customers.
