八水氢氧化钡Barium Hydroxide Octahydrate

八水氢氧化钡，分子式 Ba(OH)2·8H2O，CAS 号 12230-71-6，可用于有机合成、钡盐制造、水软化、玻璃陶瓷等行业。Barium Hydroxide Octahydrate, formula Ba(OH)2·8H2O, CAS No. 12230-71-6, is used in organic synthesis, barium salt manufacturing, water softening, glass and ceramic industries.

产品介绍Product Introduction

八水氢氧化钡可用于内燃机润滑油添加剂、钡基润滑脂和油品精制性能添加剂，也可用于甜菜糖、医药、树脂稳定剂、有机合成、其他钡盐制造、水软化以及玻璃和陶瓷工业。Barium Hydroxide Octahydrate is used for internal combustion engine lubricating oil additives, barium-based grease, and oil refining performance additives. It can also be used in beet sugar, medicine, resin stabilizers, organic synthesis, manufacturing of other barium salts, water softening, and the glass and ceramic industries.

包装、搬运与储存Packing, Handling & Storage

通常采用 50kg 或 25kg 净重塑料、纸塑复合、多层纸袋并配塑料内衬包装，也可根据客户要求安排不同包装。按照 8 Class - UN3262 危险品配载管理。储存于阴凉、干燥、通风处，保持容器密闭，防止物理损坏，并与不相容物质隔离。Usually packed in 50kg or 25kg net plastic, paper-plastic composite, or multi-layer paper bags with plastic liner. Other packing can be arranged according to customer requirements. Managed as 8 Class - UN3262 dangerous goods. Store in a tightly closed container in a cool, dry, ventilated area, protect against physical damage, and isolate from incompatible substances.
